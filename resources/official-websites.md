## Verification rules

Before changing a website from “Pending” to “Verified”:

1. Confirm that the site identifies itself as the official brand website.
2. Check that the domain is linked from an official social profile or packaging source where possible.
3. Avoid distributor, retailer, marketplace, or fan-created websites.
4. Record the date checked in the pull request or commit message.
