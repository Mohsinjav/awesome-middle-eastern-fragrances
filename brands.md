# Brand Categories

## Traditional Arabian Perfumery

These brands have a strong heritage in traditional Middle Eastern perfumery and frequently feature materials such as:

- Oud
- Rose
- Amber
- Musk
- Incense
- Saffron
- Resins

Examples include:

- Ajmal
- Al Haramain
- Arabian Oud
- Rasasi
- Swiss Arabian

---

## Contemporary Middle Eastern Brands

These brands combine traditional Middle Eastern fragrance styles with contemporary trends.

Examples include:

- Lattafa
- Maison Alhambra
- Afnan
- Armaf
- Paris Corner
- Fragrance World
- Ahmed Al Maghribi
