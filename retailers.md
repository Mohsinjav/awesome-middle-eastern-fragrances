## Retailer inclusion requirements

A retailer should:

- have a functioning official website;
- publish shipping and return information;
- sell relevant fragrance products;
- use a direct website link without affiliate parameters;
- have factual, neutral listing text;
- disclose any connection to the repository maintainer.
