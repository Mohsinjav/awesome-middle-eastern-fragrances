# Afnan Perfumes

Afnan Perfumes is a fragrance house founded in the United Arab Emirates in 2007. The company produces fragrances for men, women, and unisex use, including eau de parfum, extrait de parfum, concentrated perfume oils, gift sets, and discovery products.

According to Afnan, the company is headquartered in the UAE and distributes its products in more than 120 countries. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

- **Country:** United Arab Emirates
- **Founded:** 2007
- **Headquarters:** United Arab Emirates
- **Product categories:** Eau de parfum, extrait de parfum, perfume oils, gift sets, and discovery sets
- **Official global website:** [afnan.com](https://afnan.com/)
- **Official U.S. website:** [us.afnan.com](https://us.afnan.com/)
- **Official company profile:** [About Afnan](https://uae.afnan.com/pages/about-us)

## Overview

Afnan's catalog combines contemporary, oriental, woody, floral, fresh, gourmand, oud, musk, leather, and amber fragrance styles.

The company's official stores organize products into multiple collections and formats. Product availability may vary by country and over time.

## Selected fragrance profile

### 9 PM

9 PM is a men's fragrance from Afnan's 9 Collection.

The official product page describes it as a composition combining fresh bergamot and pear with mint, lavender, cinnamon, amber, and vanilla characteristics. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

**Officially listed notes**

- **Top:** Bergamot, lavandin, cinnamon, apple
- **Heart:** Muguet and orange blossom
- **Base:** Patchouli, amber, vanilla, and tonka bean

> Note lists can vary between regional official websites. When updating this page, use the current global product page and record the date checked.
