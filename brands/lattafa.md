# Lattafa Perfumes

Lattafa Perfumes is a family-run fragrance company established in the United Arab Emirates. The company describes itself as a third-generation business with Emirati roots and a portfolio that combines traditional fragrance influences with contemporary product development. Lattafa states that its products are available in more than 120 countries. 

- **Country:** United Arab Emirates
- **Company type:** Family-run fragrance house
- **Primary categories:** Eau de parfum, perfume oils, body products, gift sets, and home-fragrance products
- **Official website:** [lattafa.com](https://lattafa.com/)
- **Official brand catalog:** [Lattafa products](https://lattafa.com/brands/lattafa/)
- **Official company profile:** [About Lattafa](https://lattafa.com/about-us/)

## Overview

Lattafa produces fragrances for men, women, and unisex use. Its catalog includes sweet gourmands, amber fragrances, woody compositions, florals, fresh aromatics, spicy fragrances, oud-focused products, and contemporary blends.

According to the company, its product-development process includes in-house creation, design, research, development, and manufacturing.

## Selected fragrance profiles

### Khamrah

Khamrah is a unisex aromatic-spicy fragrance launched in 2022.

**Official notes**

- **Top:** Bergamot, cinnamon, nutmeg
- **Heart:** Dates accord, lily of the valley, praline, tuberose
- **Base:** Roasted tonka bean, benzoin, myrrh, Akigalawood, vanilla, amber wood

[View the official Khamrah page](https://lattafa.com/product/khamrah/)

### Khamrah Qahwa

Khamrah Qahwa is a unisex fragrance launched in 2023.

**Official notes**

- **Top:** Ginger, cinnamon, cardamom
- **Heart:** Praline, candied fruits, white flowers
- **Base:** Coffee Arabica, tonka bean, musk, benzoin, vanilla

[View the official Khamrah Qahwa page](https://lattafa.com/product/khamrah-qahwa/)

### Khamrah Dukhan

Khamrah Dukhan is a smoky variation in the Khamrah collection.

**Official notes**

- **Top:** Mandarin, pimento, smoked spices
- **Heart:** Cistus, orange flower, incense, patchouli
- **Base:** Tobacco, amber, tonka bean, benzoin, praline

[View the official Khamrah Dukhan page](https://lattafa.com/product/khamrah-dukhan/)

### Yara

Yara is a fragrance for women launched in 2020.

**Official notes**

- **Top:** Tangerine, heliotrope, orchid
- **Heart:** Tropical notes, gourmand accord
- **Base:** Vanilla, sandalwood, musk

[View the official Yara page](https://lattafa.com/product/yara/)
