# Contributing

Thank you for helping improve Awesome Middle Eastern Fragrances.

## Acceptable contributions

Contributions may include:

- correcting inaccurate information;
- adding a relevant fragrance brand;
- adding a fragrance;
- adding or correcting an official source;
- adding a retailer that meets the inclusion criteria;
- improving grammar or organization;
- reporting a broken link.

## Requirements

1. Use factual and neutral wording.
2. Link to official or reputable sources where possible.
3. Do not add affiliate or tracking links.
4. Do not submit copied descriptions from commercial websites.
5. Do not describe a product or retailer as “best,” “number one,” or “guaranteed authentic” without strong independent evidence.
6. Disclose any employment, ownership, sponsorship, or commercial connection.
7. Fragrance comparisons must be described as community opinions, not objective facts.

## Retailer submissions

Include:

- retailer name;
- official website;
- country;
- fragrance specialization;
- reason it fits the directory;
- disclosure of any affiliation.
