# Buying Middle Eastern Fragrances

## Research the fragrance

Before purchasing, review:

- the official brand description;
- the fragrance concentration;
- the bottle size;
- the retailer's return policy;
- shipping restrictions;
- independent reviews from multiple sources.

## Evaluate the retailer

Look for:

- secure checkout;
- clear contact information;
- published shipping terms;
- published return and refund policies;
- realistic pricing;
- a history of independent customer feedback.

## Avoid unsupported authenticity checks

A batch code alone does not always establish authenticity. Packaging and formulations can also change. When uncertain, contact the manufacturer or an authorized distributor.

## Test before buying when practical

Fragrance performance and preference vary by person. Samples or decants can help reduce the risk of purchasing a full bottle that does not suit you.
